POLYMORPHISM:
*POLY means many, morph means forms(many forms)
*An entity behaving differently in different situations is known as polymorphism.
TWO TYPES
1)COMPILE TIME POLYMORPHISM
2)RUNTIME POLYMORPHISM

1)COMPILE TIME POLYMORPHISM:
*Calling an overloaded method resolved at compile time based on parameters.
*method overloading is a ex of CTP.

2)RUNTIME POLYMORPHISM:
*Calling an overridden method resolved at runtime based on object type.
*method overriding is an example of RTP
