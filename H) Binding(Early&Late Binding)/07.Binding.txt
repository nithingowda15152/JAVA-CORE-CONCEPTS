BINDING:
Method call connecting to its implementation is known as Binding.

Types
1)Early Binding
2)Late Binding

EARLY BINDING:
*Method call connecting to its implementation at compile time based on reference.
*Early Binding is known as compile time binding
*Early Binding is exhibited by all the static vars ,Non-static vars ,static methods ,final methods & private methods.


LATE BINDING:
*Method call connecting to its implementation at  Run-time based on object.
*Late Binding is known as Runtime Binding.
*Late Binding is exhibited by non-static method which is not final/static.
